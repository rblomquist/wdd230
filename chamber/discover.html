Discover Sanford

At the heart of Lee County in beautiful NC Sanford is a small but tight-knit community. We may seem small and a bit worn on the outside, but inside we are full of a sense of community that we pride ourselves in.

Kiwans Park is but one of many wonderful parks in the area. It's a great place to bring the family and spend time with family and friends!

Kiwanis Park also boasts a fun-filled spalsh pad for those hot summer days to enjoy some fun in the sun!

Being founded in 1874, downtown Sanford offeres a rustic look from an older time. Many locally owned stores call this historic are their home.

Along with all the historic sites there are many nature preserves and outdoor hiking as well. White Pines Nature reserve boasts 275 acres to explore and discover!
