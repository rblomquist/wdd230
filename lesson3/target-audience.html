Target Audience

The chamber of commerce is a place where local business can come together, resident of Sanford can learn about events and other happenings going on in the community, and visitors can learn what Sanfrod is all about.


There are three main targets for this website: Sanford residents, Sanford visitors, and Sanford business owners.


  • Business Owners

    Communities are stronger when they are united. By participating with the chamber business are better able to band together and support each other


  • Residents

    There are many community events that are going on that people may or may not know about. By interacting with the chamber the local populace is able to know what is going on in their community and support local business.


  • Visitors

    Sanford doesn't get very many visitors, but there is still plenty to offer those who do. By visiting the local chamber people coming in can see all the uniqueness Sanford has to offer and enjoy that small town feel.



Scenarios

Sandra's donuts are the best donuts in town and all the locals know that! One such local recently had to close his diner as more and more chain food companies have been moving into the area taking away business from his shop. Being a local herself she was saddened to hear of the loss of yet another local business. She decided to join the local chamber of commerce to see what she could do to help protect other local businesses from meeting the same fate.


From the website Sandra hopes to:

  • Network with other business owners in the community.

  • Work with other said business owners and civil leaders to protect local interests.

  • Promote own business to continue to provide the best donuts to the local populace.


Bob and Alice are new to the area. They have finally built up the courage to get to know their neighbors and they quickly become friends. Bob and Alice have recently moved from the big city where everyone mostly keeps tho themselves and are somewhat disconnected from the community. They are surprised to hear all the events their neighbors attend and wonder where they can friends local communities they ma be interested in joining. They are directed to the chamber of commerce that has listings of community events are resources.


From the website Bob and Alice hope to:

  • Find community events in areas that interest them.

  • Feel a sense of belonging they lacked in the more "distant" community.
